I am Darren Ng, a first class theoretical physics graduate from the University of Bristol with a focus in quantum computation. On this site, you will find my academic CV, research and my Github repositories which contains any past projects that I have worked on. For my final year project, I worked on the error characterisation of non-Clifford quantum gates for a single qubit by the randomised benchmarking protocol.
